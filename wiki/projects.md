## Umeå Together
**Type:** Process facilitation, project management, branding, web development, community/crowdfunding consulting
**Client:** Umeå Kommun + Coompanion
**Status:** Completed

A social incubator for Umeå Kommun. Parti Design designed and delivered the full programme: facilitated workshops, developed the website, ran the project, designed branding, and consulted on community building and local crowdfunding using Open Collective. Supported local initiative-takers to develop their ideas and get them funded. This project directly led to the creation of the Massvis venture.

## Ventures (Parti Design Venture Studio)

These are projects incubated by Parti Design — not client commissions, but initiatives Parti Design has originated and is developing. They generate work for the studio as they mature.

Implementation note:
- Each venture can carry its own theme color in Payload (`lime`, `lavender`, or `ink`) and that accent is used on venture cards and venture detail pages.

### Massvis
**Type:** Digital platform / community finance
**Status:** Active, ongoing

A local crowdfunding and community management platform based on Open Collective, born out of Umeå Together. Currently hiring Parti Design for administrative and project management support. Long-term potential as a platform for byggemenskap housing collectives to manage shared finances and community communications.

### Umeå Kallbad
**Type:** Community sauna / outdoor bathing project
**Status:** Active

A community-led kallbadhus (cold water bathing facility) initiative. Currently hiring Parti Design to run co-building workshops for creating the sauna and surrounding area.

### DIT Egnahem (Do It Together Self-Build Housing)
**Type:** Architecture, self-build housing, community development
**Status:** In development

The first Wikihouse project in Sweden. Long-term potential to generate a large volume of work for Parti Design: project development, design work, facilitation of byggemenskap processes, co-design and co-building for community-based organisations.

## Strategic pipeline / emerging focus

**Byggemenskap (community self-build housing)**
Kasimir is in conversations with Coompanion and another studio about forming **Egnahems Fabriken i Västerbotten** — a collaboration to promote byggemenskap and co-housing, especially in rural development. Also in conversations with Byggemenskaps föreningen about developing a Swedish digital platform similar to [crowdbuilding.nl](https://crowdbuilding.nl) to connect people who want to build together locally.
