# Business Overview

## What is Parti Design?

Parti Design is a multidisciplinary architecture and design studio with a focus on regenerative placemaking and participatory design. It also operates a venture studio that incubates projects aligned with its mission.

Founded in 2022 by Kasimir and Karina — a married couple who both hold a Master's in Architecture from Umeå University — the studio was started while they were still studying, as a vessel to carry their diverse creative, research, and community interests into practice.

The name carries three meanings intentionally:
- A playful pushback against the self-serious culture of architecture ("architects doing architecture for architects")
- A reference to **participatory design** — a core methodology of the practice
- A reference to **Parti Pris** — the French term for the concept diagram that drives an architectural idea

## Who we are

**Kasimir** — Co-founder. Focuses on co-design, workshop facilitation, UX/UI design, digital services development, architecture, and regenerative development. Originally from New York City.

**Karina** — Co-founder. Focuses on graphic design, branding, architecture, and spatial design.

Both are expats living and working in Umeå, Sweden. All client communication is in English. The studio supplements with an external team of specialists brought in as needed.

## Services

Parti Design works holistically across disciplines. Services are not a menu to pick from — they are frequently combined within a single project:

- **Architecture & spatial design** — from private residences to public spaces
- **Graphic design & branding**
- **UX/UI design & digital service development** — websites, web shops, platforms
- **Co-design & co-building workshops** — community-led processes for design and construction
- **Workshop facilitation & project management** — for strategic and placemaking processes
- **Regenerative placemaking consulting** — integrating community, ecology, and local knowledge
- **Research & development** — new knowledge and digital solutions for local/regional development, planning, local economic models, and investment strategies

A typical project combines several of these. For example, Umeå Together combined workshop facilitation, project management, website development, branding, and crowdfunding strategy in one engagement.

## Philosophy

Parti Design's core belief is that humans are part of nature — and that all human creations (cities, neighbourhoods, economies) are living organisms. They resemble ecosystems: they grow, adapt, and can thrive or collapse. Designing through that lens means creating processes and systems that can keep evolving after the project ends, not just delivering a finished product.

Key principles:
- **Regenerative over sustainable** — aim for net positive impact, not just "do less harm"
- **Participatory** — local knowledge and community involvement are essential, not optional
- **Living processes** — outcomes should be open, adaptive, and able to grow
- **Resilience and diversity** — embrace local culture as a source of strength
- **Propositional** — actively generating ideas and pitching projects, not waiting for briefs

A guiding quote from Gregory Bateson:
> *"All problems arise from the difference between how we think the world works and how it really does."*

## Who we will not work with

The filter is pragmatic, not ideological: Parti Design will not deliver a bad solution, so they only work with clients who want a genuinely good one.

They will turn down work that:
- Harms people or the planet for private gain
- Requires delivering a solution they do not believe in

They are **open** to working with oil companies, large polluters, or developers with a poor public image — if those clients are genuinely committed to changing their approach and making a positive difference.

## Location & reach

- **Based in:** Umeå, Sweden
- **Local projects:** Mostly Umeå and Västerbotten region — place-based work (architecture, workshops, placemaking)
- **Remote/international clients:** Primarily for digital services and graphic design/branding (including clients from New York)

## Stage of business

Active practice since 2022 with a completed portfolio spanning architecture, facilitation, branding, digital services, and venture projects. All work to date has come through active networking, pitching, and personal referrals — no inbound leads yet. The website is being built in part to change that.

For internally describing venture maturity in the regenerative venture studio, use the shared stage language in [venture-stages.md](./venture-stages.md): `Seed`, `Root`, `Sprout`, `Grow`, and `Flourish`.

## Tone & brand voice

Approachable, creative, and playful — but also competent, trustworthy, and professional.

The tension to hold: feel like an innovative, human, unconventional studio while also communicating the reliability and credibility that large institutional clients (municipalities, property developers) need to feel before committing.
