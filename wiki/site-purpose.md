# Site Purpose

## Why this site exists

Parti Design has won all work to date through active networking, pitching, and personal relationships — zero inbound leads. The website serves two primary functions:

1. **Credibility leave-behind** — after a meeting or conversation, give people somewhere to go to read more, confirm their impression, and feel confident before committing
2. **Inbound lead generation** — for the first time, allow the studio to be discovered by people who don't already know Kasimir personally

A secondary but important function:
3. **Reduce manual follow-up** — currently a lot of time goes into repeating the same explanations via personal communication. The site should answer "what do you do?" so Kasimir doesn't have to every time.

## What success looks like

- A visitor comes away **impressed and clear** — they understand what Parti Design is, what it offers, and how it could help them specifically
- A visitor who was referred or met Kasimir feels **confident enough to reach out** without needing another long explanation
- New inbound enquiries, especially for:
  - **Digital solutions** (websites, platforms, digital services)
  - **Regenerative placemaking** (co-design, co-building, workshop facilitation, consulting)
- Institutional clients (municipalities, developers) feel they are looking at a **reliable, established practice** — not a scrappy new studio
- The venture studio projects are visible and understandable, potentially attracting **public funding or collaborative partners**

## Key goals

1. Communicate clearly what Parti Design does across its full range of disciplines
2. Demonstrate competence and credibility through a portfolio of real projects
3. Tell the regenerative placemaking story in a way that is compelling to municipalities and institutions
4. Make the venture studio legible — show that Parti Design is propositional and drives development, not just responds to briefs
5. Surface byggemenskap as a focus area and attract relevant partners and clients
6. Create a foundation for inbound discovery (SEO, referral landing, press)

## Non-goals

- This is not a Swedish-language site (for now)
- This is not a blog or news publication
- This is not trying to appeal to everyone — it has a clear values position
- It is not designed to attract clients who want cheap or fast without care for impact

## Key CTAs (calls to action)

- Get in touch / start a conversation (primary)
- Learn about a specific service or project area
- Explore the venture studio and its projects
- Understand how the studio works and what collaboration looks like

## Content priorities (rough order)

1. Who we are and what we stand for — immediately clear on landing
2. What we do — services, explained in human terms, with examples
3. Portfolio — real projects, showing breadth and the cross-disciplinary approach
4. Venture studio — Massvis, Umeå Kallbad, DIT Egnahem; the byggemenskap opportunity
5. Contact / working with us

## Byggemenskap as a strategic focus

This deserves special attention on the site. Byggemenskap (community self-build housing cooperatives) is an area where Parti Design can offer an end-to-end service:

- Setting up the organisation
- Developing strategy
- Co-designing the building with members
- Running co-building workshops for community connection
- Building the website and digital infrastructure
- Ongoing community management via Massvis (collective finance, communications)

Kasimir is actively in conversations with Coompanion, Byggemenskaps föreningen, and others about forming **Egnahems Fabriken i Västerbotten** — a collaboration to promote byggemenskap and co-housing in rural development. There is also interest in developing a Swedish digital platform (similar to crowdbuilding.nl) to connect people who want to build together.

The site should position Parti Design as a knowledgeable and mission-aligned partner in this space.
